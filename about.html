Helping Homeless

This website will include the history of homeless population in Newark, New Jersey. You will also be able to find resources that will help homeless people. My goal for this website is to inform people about Newark's homelessness.

Please visit the SERVICES tab to find out different services Newark has to offer. You will find many amazing resources to help you out. If you are homeless and need a place to stay or need food, you will find all the details on this page.
